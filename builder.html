Powered by the free Aladhan API. Don't know your coordinates? Right-click your spot on OpenStreetMap → “Show address”.

Self-configuring embeds & privacy: coarse auto-detect is approximate and IP/timezone-based (no permission prompt) — it sends the viewer’s IP to GeoJS (this widget itself does no tracking). “Precise location” uses the browser’s permission prompt and may require the iframe’s allow="geolocation" (some hosts, including TablissNG, may strip it — coarse detect still works). Saved settings stay in the viewer’s own browser/profile and may be blocked by strict privacy/third-party-storage settings — the widget keeps working either way (session-only).
